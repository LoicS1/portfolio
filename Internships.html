City of Dublin Public Works Internship
July-August 2018

During second semester junior year, I applied to a variety of internships. Although my main interest was the environmental field, I also wanted to explore related fields of engineering and have an impact in my local community, leading me to apply for a transportation engineering internship through the Bay Area's Metropolitan Transportation Commission (MTC). After navigating a comprehensive application process, including a resume, cover letter, essay, letter of rec and 20 minute interview, I selected for a full-time summer position in transportation engineering through the City of Dublin Public Works Department.

Through this internship, I was exposed to various facets of transportation engineering and related fields such as civil, structural, and water resources engineering. As a novice in the field, I was quickly exposed to basic technical knowledge, such as the MUTCD documentation on traffic signs and creating pivot table on cost analyses for construction projects in Excel.
